Sean Combs (also known as Diddy) is currently embroiled in serious legal battles. As of September 2024, he is facing a wide array of charges, including sex trafficking, racketeering, and other federal offenses. These charges stem from allegations spanning more than a decade, with claims of abuse, coercion, and exploitation of women at notorious “Freak Off” parties, often using violence and threats. Combs has been denied bail and is currently detained while awaiting trial. 4 5

The public allegations have grown in scope, with more than 50 victims reportedly coming forward, some claiming to have been minors when the abuse occurred 6
